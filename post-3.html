Tiny, a 10-year-old cat, was abandoned with his sibling in a shelter.

Because the cats were not particularly bonded to each other, they might be taken in by different homes.

The shelter's website has a note next to a picture of this cat indicating as much.

However, no one seemed in a rush to show Tiny affection. So our hero was heartbroken as a result.

Meanwhile, on the opposite side of town, a youngster named Easton was looking at the shelter's website.

Because his mother permitted him to keep one pet, the truth of the matter is. If it's a kitten, it may be a puppy.

Easton saw Tiny for the first time. Love at first sight was the case.

Easton and his mother rushed to the shelter as soon as they woke up in the morning to meet Tiny.

The encounter was delightful, thanks in large part to the cat's obvious devotion.

In the end, it turned out to be Easton who was his friend and companion.

Tiny stayed close to his new owner and purred louder than the car's engine the entire ride home.

They've become inseparable.

They are always in one other's company, even while they are sleeping.

That they were able to discover each other is a wonderful thing for both of them.
